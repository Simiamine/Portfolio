"Bouche Cousue"

Wordle game with FreePascal

Game rules:

  • Try to guess the mystery word. You have 6 attempts to find it.
  • You can only use common nouns found in the dictionary.
  • Letters surrounded by a red square are correctly placed.
  • Letters surrounded by a yellow square are present but in the wrong position.
  • Letters that remain in blue background are not in the word.
  • This game is based on the famous game Motus and the English version, Wordle.
